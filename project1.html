Computer Vision-Based Lighting System with Face Angle Detection - Final Year Research Project

Final year Research and Development project: Wayamba University of Sri Lanka
Supervisor: Mrs Lakmini Wanninayaka

Artificial lighting accounts for around 1/6 to 1/5 of total generated electricity in the world and it is continuously growing.

Efficiency of the lighting has improved significantly in the past few decades(Incandescent → Florescent lighting → LED).

But the effectiveness of the lighting has come to attention in recent years.

Vision based lighting control is a highly flexible technique which can be used in a range of applications.

Aim of the projects

To save the electricity by increasing the effectiveness of lighting through an automatic light control system with facing angle and position estimation
